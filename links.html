  • Open sales

    1. Go to edit sales form
    2. Delete the first section, which should have title "Uniform Sales are now closed".

  • Close sales

    1. Go to edit sales form
    2. Create a new section at the top of the page with title "Uniform Sales are now closed" and description "Please look out for announcements via class reps for future sales. Submitting this form will have no effect"
    3. Change the action for the new section to "Submit form"
